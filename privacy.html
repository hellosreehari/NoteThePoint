Your Data Stays With You

NoteThePoint is a "serverless" application. This means we do not have a backend database, and your notes are never sent to our servers. All your content is stored directly in your browser's Local Storage.

What We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. We don't ask for your name, email address, or phone number. There are no accounts to create and no passwords to manage.

Analytics

We use basic, privacy-respecting analytics (like Google Analytics) to understand how many people visit the site and which features are used. This data is anonymized and does not include the content of your notes.

Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties. Since we don't have your data in the first place, it's impossible for us to share it.

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
