Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?

Yes. NoteThePoint is completely free to use. No hidden subscriptions, no paid tiers.

Where are my notes stored?

Your notes are stored in your browser's Local Storage. This means they are saved on your computer or phone, and they are never sent to a server.

What happens if I clear my browser history?

If you choose to "Clear browsing data" and include "Cookies and other site data," your notes will be deleted. We recommend regularly backing up important notes by copying them to another location.

Can I access my notes on another device?

No. Since the data stays on your device, it doesn't automatically sync between your phone and your laptop. This is a trade-off we made to ensure total privacy and zero-login speed.

How do I back up my notes?

The easiest way is to use the "Copy to Clipboard" button on any note and paste it into a safe place. You can also print your notes or save them as PDFs using the print icon.
